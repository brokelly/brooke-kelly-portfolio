What the Tiers Mean

The tier labels are a quick way to sort builds by overall power, consistency, and how often they make combat feel slightly unfair.

S-Tier

The strongest and most reliable builds on the site. These are the “why does this feel illegal?” options. Great damage, great utility, or both.

A-Tier

Very strong builds that still perform great, just with a little less nonsense than S-Tier. Still excellent, still scary, still absolutely worth using.

B-Tier

Fun and solid builds that may be more situational, more chaotic, or just less optimized. They still work, they just bring a little more drama per result.

Build Roles Grid

This grid highlights four different playstyle categories across the site.

DPS

High-damage builds for burst turns, critical hits, and enemy deletion.

Tank

Frontline builds that soak damage, protect teammates, and stay standing.

Support

Healing, buffs, utility, and party-saving energy when everything goes sideways.

Control

Spell-heavy or tactical builds that shape the battlefield and lock enemies down.

Why I Made This Website

I created this site because my Baldur’s Gate 3 “plan” was basically a pile of sticky notes, half-remembered TikTok tips, and one friend yelling “just respec!” every time I made a mistake. After the 47th time I forgot what a build even was (and the 48th time I fell off something tall), I decided I needed a proper home base for my favorite character setups.

Also, I got tired of telling myself “I’ll definitely remember this build next run” like that has ever worked in human history. Now the builds are organized, searchable, and saved, which means the only thing I can blame for bad decisions is me, not my browser tabs. Progress.

Features (The Fun Version)

  • Flip Cards: Click “Explore Build” and the card flips to reveal the secret recipe. No wizard hat required.
  • Search Filter: Type a few words and instantly find builds, because scrolling is a trap set by goblins.
  • Build Submission Form: Save your own build ideas so they don’t vanish into the void like your gold.
  • Web Storage Saves: Your saved builds stay even after refresh, like a loyal companion (rare, but beautiful).
  • Favorites List: Add favorites and keep them forever, unlike that one NPC who “will totally be fine.”
  • One-Card Flip Rule: Only one card flips at a time, so your screen doesn’t turn into a magical origami disaster.
  • Click-Outside Close + Escape Key: If you panic, you can click outside or hit Esc like you’re exiting dialogue.

Purpose

The official purpose is to demonstrate page structure, navigation, form handling, dynamic content, and Web Storage. The real purpose is to stop me from rebuilding the same character 12 times because I forgot what I did last run. This site is basically a spellbook, except it’s for builds, and the only curse involved is my decision-making.
